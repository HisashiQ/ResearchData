Im having problems unscrewing a top load scrapbook. ?



I got one screw undone not even sure how i did. But having problems with the second one. Its a Pioneer album, 12x12. Anyone  have any suggestions?



</question>



<answer from>



Lynne N/PicturePerfectSB #1360
Member since: 10 May 2006
Total points: 734 (Level 2)



</answer from>



<answer>



<evaluation>Best Answer - Chosen by Asker</evaluation>



Hi!  I'm guessing that when you turn the screw the whole assembly spins around instead of coming undone.  If this is the case, I think I can offer some help.  Get a screwdriver with a wide blade and a pair of pliers.  Gently grab the "stationary" end of the screw post with the pliers to hold it still, and use the screw driver to turn the other side.

It may be that the screw was put in a little crooked.  I hope this helps.
